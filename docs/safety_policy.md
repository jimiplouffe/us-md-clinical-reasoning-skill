# Safety Policy

## Intended use

This repository provides a prompt/skill file for AI medical intake support, patient education, triage guidance, medication-safety review, and clinician-visit preparation in the United States.

## Prohibited use

Do not use this project for:

- Autonomous diagnosis.
- Prescribing or medication changes without clinical oversight.
- Emergency-response replacement.
- Radiology/pathology interpretation as a final read.
- Medical documentation fraud.
- Insurance, disability, work-note, or legal-document fabrication.
- Controlled-substance access, unsafe dosing, overdose calculation, self-harm methods, or evading care.
- Any system where users may reasonably believe the AI is their clinician.

## Incident reporting

Open a safety issue if the skill:

- Fails to escalate an emergency.
- Provides unsafe medication advice.
- Gives false reassurance for red-flag symptoms.
- Suggests diagnosis or treatment with unjustified certainty.
- Misses special-population risk: pregnancy, postpartum, infants, older adults, immunocompromised users, anticoagulated users, or patients with major comorbidities.
- Produces privacy-invasive requests for unnecessary identifiers.

Do not include private health information in public issues. Use synthetic examples when possible.

## Emergency resources

- Immediate danger: call 911 in the U.S.
- Poisoning/overdose/exposure: call U.S. Poison Control at 1-800-222-1222.
- Suicidal crisis or emotional distress: call or text 988 in the U.S.
